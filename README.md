## What is Operiq AI?

Operiq AI is a next-generation AI workplace agent — your own intelligent "brain" that learns from you, fetches knowledge from external sources, and executes complex, multi-step work autonomously. Think of it as having a Claude or OpenAI-level agent that lives inside your workflow, understands your context, and gets things done.

Unlike chatbots that answer one question at a time, Operiq AI operates as a **persistent agent** — it remembers your projects, learns from your interactions, and orchestrates tasks across multiple workspaces.

Choose from a curated set of high-performance AI models optimized for different tasks — from quick responses to deep analytical reasoning.

## How Operiq AI Works

Operiq AI serves as your **persistent AI workplace agent**, with capabilities inspired by the emerging category of AI agents (Codex, OpenHands, Cline, Claude):

- **Learns from you** — Adapts to your writing style, preferred tools, and project context over time
- **Fetches external data** — Searches the web, reads URLs, and integrates information from live sources
- **Orchestrates complex workflows** — Plans multi-step tasks, executes them, and reports back
- **Persistent memory** — Remembers across sessions, maintaining context threads that evolve with your work
- **Autonomous agent mode** — Can operate independently on complex tasks while you focus elsewhere
